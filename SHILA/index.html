Peach Cake

This peach cake is a delightful combination of cuteness and deliciousness. The soft and moist layers of cake are infused with the natural sweetness of ripe peaches, tantalizing your taste buds with every bite. Topped with a luscious cream frosting and adorned with fresh peach slices, it's a visually charming treat that will leave you craving for more.

Strawberry cake

This small strawberry cake is absolutely adorable with its petite size and charming presentation. It exudes a delightful aroma of fresh strawberries, and the moist sponge cake layered with luscious strawberry cream is a true melt-in-your-mouth experience. Every bite is bursting with the sweet and tangy flavors of the perfectly ripe strawberries, making it a truly delicious treat for any dessert lover.

Cherry cake

This cherry cake is not only cute but also incredibly delicious. The beautiful red cherries add a pop of color that instantly grabs your attention. With every bite, you'll experience the perfect balance of moist cake and sweet, juicy cherries. It's the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth and brighten up any occasion.
